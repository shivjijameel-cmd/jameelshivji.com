About

About

I’m a South Asian Ismaili Muslim screenwriter, actor, and entrepreneur with a passion for marketing, technology, and dark comedy.

Translation: I’m superrr Californian. At least in the cultural melting-pot sense.

Dad from East Africa, mom once a refugee in Italy, and both sisters Canadian-born. It’s in my DNA to embrace anything and everything new. That can look like many things, whether it's...

  • My middle school self (obese, glasses, mushroom haircut), forcing my parents to drive 6 hours to Los Angeles for weekly auditions.
  • Pitching Google Ventures and David Tisch for $1.25M as an eighteen-year-old with no prior experience in tech.
  • Buying an 8-foot-tall halal cart and throwing my back out lugging it around NYC.

Taking these risks and more fuel my screenwriting. I’m drawn to age-old cultural rifts, generational divides, and adults who age into children (perks of having a big tight-knit family).

Oh & since we’re still here, I also love boxing, Japanese menswear, indie music, advocating for equal rights, traveling, + I make a mean British trifle.

Wanna chat? Hit me up!

NYU Tisch Film & TV BFA ’26 Minor in NYU Stern Business (BEMT) Dual U.S. & Canadian citizen Based in Los Angeles & New York
